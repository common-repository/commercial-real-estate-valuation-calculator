Calculator for commercial property valuations.

== Description ==

Elevate user engagement with our dynamic Commercial Real Estate Valuation Calculator plugin. Turn your visitors into clients. This intuitive tool, an extension of the CRECalculator.com, offers instant, comprehensive analysis of commercial property Cap Rate and ROI. Users simply input Purchase Price, Net Operating Income, and Down Payment to auto-generate Loan Amount, Annual Debt Service, and Annual Cash Flow. The calculator dynamically displays Debt Service Coverage Ratio, Cap Rate, and ROI, providing valuable insights at a glance. Embed the [crevc-calculator] anywhere on your site for a seamless integration. Fully customizable, it's designed to blend perfectly with your website's aesthetics.

= Documentation =

Check out our [documentation](https://www.reallaunch.com/valuation-calculator-plugin/) for more information on functionality.

== Installation ==

Navigate to 'Plugins' and select 'Add New'. You can then choose to either search for our plugin and hit 'Install', or upload the Plugin ZIP file to your Plugin folder. After activating the plugin, you'll find its settings conveniently located under 'Settings > Valuation Calculator'. This streamlined process makes it easy to integrate our powerful tool into your site.
